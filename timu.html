Timu: Put time back into your hands

Timu is a personal time and schedule manager that stays true to your current progress. This pitch was created to complement my portoflio submission to University of Southern California's Iovine and Young Academy.

Client:

USC Iovine and Young Academy

Timeline:

  • Nov 2020 - Dec 2020

    • Process:

      My video creation process starts brainstorming all the necessary features on some good old fashioned paper. Then as I began to envision how the app would look and function, I turned to Adobe Illustrator to give it a body. Next, I imported the images into After Effects to bring it to life, before finally choosing a rhythmic soundtrack and combining it all into one cohesive video in Final Cut Pro.

    Apps Used:

    • Adobe After Effects, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Illustrator
